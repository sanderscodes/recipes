Mac and Cheese

Description

    Macaroni pasta with cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • Macaroni Pasta
  • Cheese
  • milk
  • butter
  • flour

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni.
  2. Melt butter in pan.
  3. Add flour to melted butter.
  4. Add milk to pan stir 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheese and stir till melted.
  6. Drain macaroni add to pan and stir.
